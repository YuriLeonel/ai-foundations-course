## What is Artificial Intelligence (AI)?

Artificial Intelligence is:

* A set of systems and technologies capable of solving problems and performing tasks that would normally require human intelligence.
* A field of computer science and engineering dedicated to building solutions that leverage intelligence to perform tasks.

**Example:** Audio search on smartphones (using Natural Language Processing).

---

## Origins of AI

* **1950s**: Researchers such as Alan Turing explored how to build thinking, intelligent machines.
* **Inspiration from nature**: Neural networks designed after the human nervous system.

---

## Classification of Intelligent Systems

* **Narrow AI (Weak AI):**
  Systems designed for specific tasks.
  *Examples: chatbots, computer vision systems, generative AI like ChatGPT.*

* **General AI (Strong AI):**
  A research area under development, aiming to create robots and applications capable of performing *any* intellectual task.
  *Example: humanoid robots from sci-fi movies like **Ex Machina**.*

---

## Conclusion

Artificial Intelligence is a vast field of computer science focused on creating applications and systems with traits of rationality to support decision-making.
